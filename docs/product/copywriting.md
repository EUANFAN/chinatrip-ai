## Chinese UI Direction

Chinese UI should be practical and concise. It is for users who choose Chinese as the interface language, not necessarily for Chinese domestic tourists.

## Copy Rules

- Keep product copy direct and action-oriented.
- Avoid traditional travel agency tone.
- Avoid overpromising real-time policy accuracy.
- Use "traveling in China" and "China travel" for SEO.
- Make Share and Copy visible as product value, but do not over-explain them.
